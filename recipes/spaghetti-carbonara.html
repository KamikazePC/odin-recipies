Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish that's simple and delicious. Spaghetti Carbonara is known for its creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese, and pancetta or bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
