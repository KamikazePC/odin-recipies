Grilled Chicken Salad

This healthy salad features grilled chicken, fresh greens, and a zesty vinaigrette dressing. It's a perfect choice for a light and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 5 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • For the vinaigrette: olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper
